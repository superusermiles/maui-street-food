## Issues Found
- **Home page:** The homepage is materially incomplete versus the brief and design spec. It renders only four text sections with no hero image, no CTA buttons, no featured truck cards, no events teaser card layout, no visit CTA strip, and none of the layered visual treatment required for the primary landing page.
- **Home page:** No imagery is shown on the live homepage even though the brief and design spec require the homepage to create a strong first impression with multiple food and atmosphere images.
- **Home page / responsive QA:** Because the homepage is reduced to plain text blocks, the live result does not meet the expected responsive visual experience shown in the design spec across desktop, tablet, and mobile.
- **Footer:** The footer is missing direct contact information. It includes hours and address, but no phone number or email/contact method, so the footer is not complete with contact info.
- **Find Us page:** The enquiry success message "Enquiry sent. We’ll get back to you soon." is shown before any submission. This is misleading state/content on initial page load.

## Verdict
The live site is up, the four required routes exist, navigation works, and the inner pages are substantially stronger than the homepage. Branding, typography, color palette, logo, favicon, and baseline metadata are in place. However, this is still a FAIL because the homepage—the most important page in the brief—is significantly underbuilt compared with the required content and visual structure, and the footer is missing complete contact details. The site needs a fully realized homepage and a corrected footer before final approval.
